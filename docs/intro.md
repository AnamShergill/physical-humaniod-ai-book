# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This AI-native textbook covers the fundamental concepts, practical implementations, and cutting-edge research in the field of humanoid robotics and physical artificial intelligence.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed to provide a thorough understanding of:
- The foundations of physical AI and its applications in humanoid robotics
- ROS 2 architecture and its role in robotic systems
- Python integration and URDF modeling
- Gazebo physics simulation for humanoid robots
- Unity visualization and interaction
- Isaac Sim SDK basics and navigation
- Hardware setup and cloud simulation

## How to Navigate

The textbook is organized into progressive chapters, each building upon the previous knowledge. Start with the Foundations of Physical AI and work your way through the various topics. Each lesson includes practical examples, code snippets, and exercises to reinforce your learning.

## Target Audience

This textbook is suitable for:
- Students studying robotics and AI
- Researchers in the field of humanoid robotics
- Engineers working on robotic systems
- Anyone interested in the intersection of AI and physical systems

## Prerequisites

To get the most out of this textbook, you should have:
- Basic programming knowledge (preferably Python)
- Understanding of fundamental mathematics (linear algebra, calculus)
- Interest in robotics and AI concepts

## Getting Started

Begin your journey by exploring the first chapter on the Foundations of Physical AI, where we'll establish the core concepts that will be built upon throughout the textbook.
